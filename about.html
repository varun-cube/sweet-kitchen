Who We Are

Sweet Kitchen started with a simple mission: to bring fresh, authentic Indian sweets and meals to Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar families. What began as a small operation has grown into a trusted cloud kitchen serving thousands of happy customers.

Every day, our dedicated team prepares fresh sweets, meals, and snacks using premium ingredients and traditional recipes. We believe that great food starts with fresh ingredients and careful preparation.

Today, we're proud to serve customers across Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar with fast delivery, competitive pricing, and uncompromising quality.
